Problems or questions? Contact us!

 

  1. Select annual payment for annual billing; otherwise payment is monthly. Prices do not include VAT. Automated invoicing and payment via credit card or debit advice. We offer a 20% attribution discount.
  2. The Free Plan is for non-commercial use only. It has a limited credit count per minute, a limited set of vehicle profiles and cannot use the flexible mode (ch.disable=true).
  3. Limits: a maximum of 10 requests per second with up to 1000 credits per minute (c/min) are available for the Premium Plan. For the Standard Plan, 2 requests per second with up to 400 c/min are available. For the Basic Plan, the maximum is 1 request per second with up to 100 c/min. Requests that violate the limits might be blocked to provide a stable service for all customers, see here for more details. The Free Plan does not include any guarantees..
  4. The SLA is 99.5% availability within each month. If the SLA reaches less than 99.5% availability (but more than 99%), we apply a 10% discount. If it reaches less than 99%, we apply a 50% discount. Higher SLA requirements can be arranged for Custom Plans.
  5. All Plans include free online support, software updates and a minimum of one weekly OpenStreetMap data update. All paid plans include email support. Premium Plans include telephone support. For a Custom Plan, a dedicated support is available for purchase. Contact us for more information.
  6. All paid plans come with the white-label option: it isn’t necessary to attribute GraphHopper, but doing so will make you eligible for the attribution discount.
